My name is Artem Nikolayev

At 22 years old, I find myself immersed in the intricate and dynamic world of web development, constantly evolving and learning in this fast-paced realm. My journey into this field is fueled by a profound passion for creating unique and aesthetically pleasing elements, a passion that extends to my work with video processing and creation using Cinema 4D and After Effects. Every project is a canvas where I blend technical acumen with artistic nuances to breathe life into visionary concepts.

My journey has been one of resilience and discovery. At the tender age of 15, I traveled alone to Israel as part of the Naale program, embracing new experiences and challenges. This journey has been instrumental in shaping my perspectives and honing my skills, allowing me to view every obstacle as an opportunity for growth.

I take immense joy in what I do, and this joy is the driving force behind every endeavor I undertake. It's not just about coding or creating visuals; it's about crafting experiences, building the unseen, and bringing ideas to life. Every line of code written and every frame created is imbued with a piece of my creativity and passion, reflecting my commitment to excellence and my love for the craft. Welcome to my space, where every pixel tells a story, and every story is a step in my ongoing journey. I hope you find inspiration and see the beauty in the unique and beautiful things I have the privilege to create.
